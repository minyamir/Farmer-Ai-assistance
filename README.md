🌱 Farmer AI Assistance

Farmer AI Assistance is an intelligent agricultural support platform designed to help farmers improve productivity and make smarter farming decisions. The system provides AI-powered crop recommendations, weather updates, disease detection, farming guidance, and modern agricultural support tools.

Features include:

* AI-based crop and farming recommendations
* Plant disease detection
* Weather forecasting
* Smart farming tips
* Farmer-friendly multilingual support
* Easy-to-use interface for local farmers

The goal of Farmer AI Assistance is to empower farmers with technology and improve sustainable agriculture through accessible digital solutions.
